Fees and Rules

Our hotel offers 3 types of luxurious rooms that you can choose according to your desired type of stay. Room fees are mentioned for your reference.

Suite

Room charge per night: $250

Deluxe Room

Room charge per night: $150

Standard Room

Room charge per night: $100

*Additional Person: $30 per night

*All fees are subject to tax and service charges. For more details on special rates and packages, please contact our reception.

Rules

  • Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 12:00 PM.
  • Smoking is not allowed inside the rooms. Designated smoking areas are provided.
  • Pets are not allowed on the premises, with the exception of service animals.
  • Cancellation policy: Please refer to the booking confirmation for details.

Thank you for choosing Starlight Hotel. We strive to make your stay comfortable and memorable.
