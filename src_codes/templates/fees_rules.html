Fees and Rules

Fees

Our hotel offers various fee structures depending on the type of room and the duration of stay. Below are the details:

  • Standard Room: $100 per night
  • Deluxe Room: $150 per night
  • Suite: $250 per night
  • Additional Person: $30 per night

All fees are subject to tax and service charges. For more details on special rates and packages, please contact our reception.

Rules

To ensure a pleasant stay for all guests, please adhere to the following rules:

  • Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 12:00 PM.
  • Smoking is not allowed inside the rooms. Designated smoking areas are provided.
  • Pets are not allowed on the premises, with the exception of service animals.
  • Any damage to hotel property will be charged to the guest's account.
  • Quiet hours are observed from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM. Please be considerate of other guests.

We appreciate your cooperation and hope you enjoy your stay with us!
